$scope

A scope is an object that houses the application model.

  • An Angular application has exactly one root scope
  • Directives are linked to a scope during compilation
  • Child scopes inherit properties prototypically from their parent
  • This forms a hierarchy that often mimics the structure of the DOM
  • Many directives create their own scope (which may or may not be isolated from their parent)
  • Expressions in the DOM are evaluated in the context of a scope
  • During linking, $watch expressions are created on scope properties. This effectively binds the view to the data model.
