Services

A service is a singleton object that is used to carry out specific tasks.

  • Services are meant to be very modular and reusable
  • Can provide common functionality to other parts of the application
  • Can be used to share context between other parts of the application
  • Angular comes with many built in services, and you can create your own
  • Services do not have a $scope
  • Controllers, Directives, and other Services can depend on a service
    • Angular's dependency injection system injects the service as a parameter to the object's Constructor
